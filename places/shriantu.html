Shree Antu

Location: Illam,Nepal

Distance from capital:498.2 km

Altitude:2,328 m


Description:

Shree Antu is a town and Village Development Committee in Ilam District in the Mechi Zone of eastern Nepal. At the time of the 1991 Nepal census it had a population of 4,452. Shree Antu is a tourist destination best known as the place to observe sunrise and also famous for tea gardens. Shree Antu Pokhari at Shree Antu town in Ilam district Nepal A view tower has been constructed atop of the Sriantu Hill situated at an altitude of 2328m from sea level.
